    Block Blast

    What is the Block Blast game?

    Block Blast is an engaging puzzle game that challenges players to strategically place different shaped blocks on an 88 grid. The goal is to complete full rows or columns, which then clear from the board, earning points and making room for additional blocks. As the game progresses, space becomes more limited, requiring careful planning and strategic thinking to keep the board from filling up.

    With simple drag-and-drop controls, Block Blast is easy to pick up but offers plenty of depth for those who enjoy a challenge. The straightforward yet strategic gameplay keeps players focused on improving their skills and achieving higher scores. Every move matters, making each decision crucial as players work to extend their run and push their limits. Whether youre looking for a relaxing way to pass the time or a serious puzzle challenge, the game provides a satisfying and rewarding experience.

    How to Play

    • Drag and drop differently shaped blocks onto the 88 grid
    • Align blocks to fill an entire row or column, which will clear from the board and earn you points
    • Clearing multiple rows or columns at once earns you combo points
    • New blocks appear after each move, so plan ahead to avoid running out of space
    • The game ends when no more blocks can be placed
